## Epidemiology — Key Metrics

Parkinson’s disease is a progressive neurodegenerative disorder affecting dopaminergic neurons in the substantia nigra. Incidence increases with age and varies across geographic populations.

### Key Risk Factors

- Aging
- Genetic susceptibility
- Environmental toxin exposure
- Oxidative stress
- Neuroinflammation
